Products

Oculus Rift

One of the most well-known VR devices. Development first began on the Rift in 2012, majorly funded by a Kickstarter program. A consumer version of the device was made available earlier this year.The Oculus Rift is primarily meant for gaming, though it can also be used for movies.

Leap Motion

The Leap Motion is a small USB device that's similar to the Kinect for Xbox 360 and Xbox 1. It captures the movement of a person's hand and send it to a host computer that analyzes and reacts to the movements. LM can be used for gaming, but can also be used for general interaction with web browsers and other software, much like using a tablet.

Google Glass

Google Glass is a head-mounted device that resembles a pair of glasses and is much smaller than the Oculus Rift. However it's not meant for gaming and is closer to augmented reality than true virtual reality. Glass has various uses, such as internet browsing, recording videos and taking pictures, checking email, getting direction, and much more.
